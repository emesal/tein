hand-written .sld — overrides macro-generated version to add regexp-fold.

all user-facing fns (regexp-search, regexp-matches, etc.) are native rust fns registered via define_fn_variadic (patch H makes them importable from the library). regexp-fold is registered separately as a hand-written native fn.
