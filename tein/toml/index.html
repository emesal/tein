(tein toml) — bidirectional TOML ↔ scheme value conversion.

TOML parsing goes through toml_crate::Value then maps to scheme Value. TOML stringifying builds a toml_crate::Value tree from raw chibi sexps then delegates to toml_crate::to_string() for correct formatting.
